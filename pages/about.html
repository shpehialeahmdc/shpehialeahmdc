About SHPE MDC Hialeah

Empowering Hispanic Engineers to Realize Their Fullest Potential

Our Mission

SHPE changes lives by empowering the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support, and development.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SHPE? +

SHPE (Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers) is a national organization that empowers Hispanic students and professionals in STEM fields through networking, leadership, and career development opportunities.

How can I get involved? +

You can join SHPE by registering at SHPE.org and connecting with our chapter through SharkNet. We welcome all students interested in STEM fields!

What projects do you work on? +

Our chapter focuses on robotics projects, including autonomous systems, computer vision, and sensor integration. We also participate in SHPE National competitions and community outreach programs.
