Frequently Asked Questions

What is the membership fee?

+

SHPE membership fee is $10 per year for students. This fee helps support chapter activities and events throughout the year.

Who can join?

+

Any MDC student in STEM fields can join, regardless of ethnicity or background. We welcome diversity and encourage all interested students to participate.

What is the time commitment?

+

We recommend attending at least two events per month to get the most out of your membership. Events include workshops, networking sessions, and project meetings.

How can I get involved?

+

After joining, you can participate in projects, attend events, and apply for leadership positions. We offer various committees and roles to match your interests and skills.
